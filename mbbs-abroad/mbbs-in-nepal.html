Frequently Asked Questions for Studying MBBS in Nepal

Does NEET Require to get Admission in Nepal?

Yes. It is required to qualify for the NEET exam before getting admission to a Nepales medical college. Some universities offer admission to MBBS for students who have scored just qualification marks in the Exam. Most of the other universities ask for minimum qualifying marks in NEET exam.

What are the minimum marks required in12th PCB to get admission in Nepales Medical College?

Candidates must score at least 50% marks in PCB ( Physics, Chemistry, Biology) and English in order to get admission in any Nepales medical college.

Do I Need to qualify IELTS and TOEFL to get MBBS admission in Nepal ?

Numerous colleges in Nepales don’t expect you to qualify either TOEFL or IELTS. Notwithstanding, a few foundations do expect you to qualify one of those tests.

What is the Fee Structure of Medical Colleges in Nepal ?

The education costs for most medical universities in Nepal is somewhere close to $8000 to $12000. The hostel expense is about $100 to $150. The hostel expense is moderately lower contrasted with the rents charged by spaces outside the colleges.

Which are Top Medical Universities in Nepal?

Based on the world universities’ Ranking database. The following universities are the top in the Nepales for Medical Courses.

  1. Kathmandu University
  2. Tribhuvan University
  3. Patan Academy of Health Sciences (PAHS)

Is MBBS from Nepal valid for practice in India ?

MCI perceives most clinical establishments in Nepal. Degrees from those establishments are legitimate for training in India. The main necessity for them is that they should qualify the MCI Screening Test and Foreign Medical Graduates Examination (FMGE) is a licensure examination conducted by the National Board of Examination (NBE) in India.
