Ukraine is becoming very among international students who want to pursue their MBBS from abroad. Ukraine has several world-class Government as well as private Medical Universities that offer MBBS, MD, and other medical degree courses.

Frequently Asked Questions for Studying MBBS in Ukraine

Does NEET Require to get Admission in Ukraine?

Yes. It is required to qualify for the NEET exam before getting admission to a Ukraine medical college. Some universities offer admission to MBBS for students who have scored just qualification marks in the Exam. Most of the other universities ask for minimum qualifying marks in NEET exam.

What are the minimum marks required in12th PCB to get admission in Ukraine Medical College?

Candidates must score at least 50% marks in PCB ( Physics, Chemistry, Biology) and English in order to get admission in any Ukraine medical college.

Do I Need to qualify IELTS and TOEFL to get MBBS admission in Ukraine ?

Numerous colleges in Ukraine don’t expect you to qualify either TOEFL or IELTS. Notwithstanding, a few foundations do expect you to qualify one of those tests.

What is the Fee Structure of Medical Colleges in Ukraine ?

The education costs for most medical universities in Ukraine is somewhere close to $6000 to $8000. The inn expense is about $160 to $260. The inn expense is moderately higher contrasted with the rents charged by spaces outside the colleges.

Which are Top 5 Medical Universities in Ukraine?

  1. Zaporizhzhia state medical university

Is MBBS from Ukraine valid for practice in India ?

MCI perceives most clinical establishments in Ukraine. Degrees from those establishments are legitimate for training in India. The main necessity for them is that they should qualify the MCI Screening Test and Foreign Medical Graduates Examination (FMGE) is a licensure examination conducted by the National Board of Examination (NBE) in India.

 

Top Medical Universities in Ukraine for MBBS

Zaporizhzhia State Medical University

Zaporizhzhia State Medical University is a medical university in the small city of Zaporizhzhia, Ukraine.

Eligibility: 50% marks in PCB followed by an Online test.
Entrance: NEET Qualification

VN Karazin Kharkiv National University

Karazin University or officially V. N. Karazin Kharkiv National University is one of the major universities in Ukraine, and earlier in the Russian Empire and the Soviet Union.

Eligibility: 50% marks in PCB
Entrance: NEET Qualification

 

Kharkiv National Medical University

Kharkiv National Medical University, formerly known as Kharkiv Medical Institute and previously Kharkiv State Medical University, is a medical university in Kharkiv, Ukraine. Over 700 teachers work at the departments of the university.

Eligibility: 60% marks in PCB with NEET Score
Entrance: NEET Qualification

 

Bogomolets National Medical University

Bogomolets National Medical University is a medical school founded in 1841 in Kyiv, Russian Empire by the Russian Tsar Nicolas I. The university is named after physiologist Alexander A. Bogomolets. NMU provides medical training for over 10,000 students, including about 1,300 foreigners from 56 countries

Eligibility: 60% marks in PCB followed by an Online test.
Entrance: NEET Qualification

 

Dnepropetrovsk State Medical Academy

The State Establishment “DNIPROPETROVSK MEDICAL ACADEMY of Health Ministry Of Ukraine” not to be confused with “Dnipro Medical Institute” was founded on September 15, 1916 from the Ekaterynoslavsky higher female courses Institute.

Eligibility: 65% marks in PCB
Entrance: NEET Qualification
