Frequently Asked Questions for Studying MBBS in Georgia

Does NEET Require to get Admission in Georgia?

Yes. It is required to qualify for the NEET exam before getting admission to a Georgian medical college. Some universities offer admission to for students who have scored just qualification marks in the Exam. Most of the other universities ask for minimum qualifying marks in NEET exam.

What are the minimum marks required in12th PCB to get admission in Georgian Medical College?

Candidates must score at least 50% marks in PCB ( Physics, Chemistry, Biology) and English in order to get admission in any Georgian medical college.

Do I Need to qualify IELTS and TOEFL to get MBBS admission in Georgia ?

Numerous colleges in Georgia don’t expect you to qualify either TOEFL or IELTS. Notwithstanding, a few foundations do expect you to qualify one of those tests.

What is the Fee Structure of Medical Colleges in Georgia ?

The education costs for most medical universities in Georgia is somewhere close to $5000 to $8000. The hostel expense is about $160 to $260. The hostel expense is moderately lower contrasted with the rents charged by spaces outside the colleges.

Which are Top 5 Medical Universities in Georgia?

Based on the world universities’ Ranking database. The following universities are the top 5 in Georgia for medical Courses.

  1. Georgian American University
  2. University of Georgia
  3. Tbilisi Medical Teaching University.
  4. Caucasus International University.
  5. Tbilisi State Medical University. 

 

Is MBBS from Georgia university valid for practice in India ?

MCI perceives most clinical establishments in Georgia. Degrees from those establishments are legitimate for training in India. The main necessity for them is that they should qualify the MCI Screening Test and Foreign Medical Graduates Examination (FMGE) is a licensure examination conducted by the National Board of Examination (NBE) in India.
