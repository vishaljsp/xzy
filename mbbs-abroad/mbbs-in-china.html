by Future Education | Aug 14, 2020

MBBS in China Overview

 CHINA is one of the Asian Countries which has become a favoured nation in the last few years for MBBS. BEIJING is the capital of this country and it is very well known for its advanced medical infrastructure and latest research. Around 45 Medical Universities in China which are MCI approved and also have been graded in various categories i.e A/A+/B/B+/C/C+ . So it is always preferable and advised to select one of the A grade universities to pursue a Medical Career. China is one of the most visited countries worldwide for its excellent tourist attractions and historical sites. Since this country shares a border with India, the culture is quite similar in terms of hospitality and fooding habits. Indian students never find any difficulty to accommodate here as indian food is easily available in the market and canteens of the universities are also provided with indian food.

As far as the cost of living and tuition fees of MBBS is concerned it is much lesser in comparison to US, UK and EUROPEAN countries even many private colleges of south india charge more than a A/A+ GRADE medical university of CHINA. One more reason for the affordable medical education in CHINA is that most of the medical universities provide scholarships so it is quite helpful in terms of financial terms. According to a study, Medical Education in CHINA is 70% cheaper than most of the EUROPEAN Countries and US as well.

MBBS in China : Eligibility Criteria

China as an education hub for Medical Education has always been demanding for the Advanced Medical Infrastructure and Research Institutions. China has set a benchmark for quality medical education along with affordable course fees and accommodations. To pursue MD (Equivalent to MBBS) in China is much like in India with low tuition fees. Medical universities in China are well recognized by the International Establishments and the MCI as well. Certain criteria has to be fulfilled to get eligible to pursue MD in china which are as follows :

  • A Candidate must not be less than 17 yrs of age and not exceeding 25 yrs. 
  • A candidate must possess 50% marks in Physics, Chemistry, Biology and English as core subjects. However candidates from Reserved category have to produce 40% marks in mentioned subjects.
  • Candidates must have qualified in with at least 60% of marks.
  • Candidates must possess all the necessary documents and certificates to the concerning authority for admissions.

WHY PURSUING MBBS IN CHINA?

  • CHINA is known for its Advanced Medical Infrastructure and Latest Research.
  • Affordable cost of living and tuition fees.
  • English is the medium of learning for all Medical Courses.
  • 45 MCI Approved Medical Universities
  • Worldwide acceptance of degree conferred by CHINESE MEDICAL UNIVERSITIES.
  • Classification of Universities in various Grades i.e A/A+, B/B+ etc
  • The passing rate of China is better than other Asian countries.

The official language of CHINA is MANDARIN but as far as the medium of Education and Course of MBBS is concerned it is english in almost all MCI approved Medical University so Language will never be a concern for any International Student. 

So CHINA being a developed country, has a much Advanced System in the field of medicine and it is still soaring up for one of its kind Research and Development in the Health Sciences and Medical Research. In all aspects CHINA is one of the best Suitable Country to pursue MBBS and one should consider this Country to fulfil his/her dream of becoming a Doctor.
