Frequently Asked Questions about  MBBS in Russia Admission 2022

Does NEET Require for study MBBS in Russia 2022?

Yes. It is required to qualify for the NEET exam before getting admission to Russian medical college. Some universities offer admission to MBBS for students who have scored just qualification marks in the Exam. Most of the other universities ask for minimum qualifying marks in NEET exam.

What are the minimum marks required in12th PCB to get admission in Russian Medical College?

Candidates must score at least 50% marks in PCB ( Physics, Chemistry, Biology) and English in order to get admission in any Russian medical college.

Do I Need to qualify IELTS and TOEFL to get MBBS admission in Russian ?

Numerous colleges in Russia don’t expect you to qualify either TOEFL or IELTS. Notwithstanding, a few foundations do expect you to qualify one of those tests.

What is the Fee Structure of Medical Colleges in Russia ?

The education costs for most medical universities in Russia is somewhere close to $4500 to $6000 for the first year. Then 2nd -6th year $3500-$5000 /yearly. The Hostel expense is about $150 to $200. The hostel expense is moderately lower contrasted with the rents charged by spaces outside the colleges.

Is MBBS from Russia valid for practice in India ?

MCI perceives most clinical establishments in Russia. Degrees from those establishments are legitimate for training in India. The main necessity for them is that they should qualify the MCI Screening Test and Foreign Medical Graduates Examination (FMGE) is a licensure examination conducted by the National Board of Examination (NBE) in India.
