Frequently Asked Questions for Studying MBBS in Uzbekistan

Does NEET Require to get Admission in Uzbekistan?

Yes. It is required to qualify for the NEET exam before getting admission to Uzbekistan medical college. Some universities offer admission to MBBS for students who have scored just qualification marks in the Exam. Most of the other universities ask for minimum qualifying marks in NEET exam.

What are the minimum marks required in12th PCB to get admission in Uzbekistan Medical College?

Candidates must score at least 50% marks in PCB ( Physics, Chemistry, Biology) and English in order to get admission in any Uzbekistan medical college.

Do I Need to qualify IELTS and TOEFL to get MBBS admission in Uzbekistan ?

Numerous colleges in Uzbekistan don’t expect you to qualify either TOEFL or IELTS. Notwithstanding, a few foundations do expect you to qualify one of those tests.

What is the Fee Structure of Medical Colleges in Uzbekistan ?

The education costs for most medical universities in Uzbekistan is somewhere close to $3000 to $5000. The inn expense is about $60 to $100. The inn expense is moderately higher contrasted with the rents charged by spaces outside the colleges.

Which are Top Medical Universities in Uzbekistan?

  1. Andijan State Medical Institute
  2. Bukhara State Medical Institute

Is MBBS from Uzbekistan valid for practice in India ?

MCI perceives most clinical establishments in Uzbekistan. Degrees from those establishments are legitimate for training in India. The main necessity for them is that they should qualify the MCI Screening Test and Foreign Medical Graduates Examination (FMGE) is a licensure examination conducted by the National Board of Examination (NBE) in India.
