Top Medical Universities in Armenia

 

Armenian Medical Institute

Armenian Medical Institute (AMI) was founded in 1990. It is the first private medical institution of higher education on the post-Soviet territory. It was accredited by the state in 2001.

Official Website | http://armedin.am/en/

University of Traditional Medicine

The University of Traditional Medicine is a leading school of medical education, medical science, and health care, which activity is aimed at training those who are aware of their role in a physician-patient relationship, with a high level of morality.

Official Website | http://www.utm.am/

Yerevan State Medical University

The Yerevan State Medical University is one of the leading public universities in Armenia. The university is established in 1920 in Yerevan. It is one of the famous among the eastern European medical universities. The Yerevan State Medical University is a part of the international association of universities, which deliver great opportunities for the student all around the globe.

Official Website | http://www.ysmu.am/

Mkhitar Gosh University

Mkhitar Gosh was founded in 1996. Currently, the Mkhitar Gosh is a modern university with a comprehensive infrastructure, and experienced faculty members that are nationally (and some of them worldwide) recognized specialists in different areas of medicine, professional health organizations, and agencies.

Official Website | http://armenianrussianinternationaluniversity.com/
