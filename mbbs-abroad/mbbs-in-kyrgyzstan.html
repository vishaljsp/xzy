Frequently Asked Questions for Studying MBBS in Kyrgyzstan

Does NEET Require to get Admission in Kyrgyzstan?

Yes. It is required to qualify for the NEET exam before getting admission to a Kyrgyzstan medical college. Some universities offer admission to MBBS for students who have scored just qualification marks in the Exam. Most of the other universities ask for minimum qualifying marks in NEET exam.

What are the minimum marks required in12th PCB to get admission in Kyrgyzstan Medical College?

Candidates must score at least 50% marks in PCB ( Physics, Chemistry, Biology) and English in order to get admission in any Kyrgyzstan medical college.

Do I Need to qualify IELTS and TOEFL to get MBBS admission in Kyrgyzstan ?

Numerous colleges in Kyrgyzstan don’t expect you to qualify either TOEFL or IELTS. Notwithstanding, a few foundations do expect you to qualify one of those tests.

What is the Fee Structure of Medical Colleges in Kyrgyzstan ?

The education costs for most medical universities in Kyrgyzstan is somewhere close to $3000 to $5000. The inn expense is about $60 to $100. The inn expense is moderately higher contrasted with the rents charged by spaces outside the colleges.

Which are Top Medical Universities in Kyrgyzstan?

Based on the world universities’ Ranking database. The following universities are the top 10 in Kyrgyzstan for Medical Courses.

  1. Osh State University

  2. Jalal-Abad State Medical University

Is MBBS from Kyrgyzstan valid for practice in India ?

MCI perceives most clinical establishments in Kyrgyzstan. Degrees from those establishments are legitimate for training in India. The main necessity for them is that they should qualify the MCI Screening Test and Foreign Medical Graduates Examination (FMGE) is a licensure examination conducted by the National Board of Examination (NBE) in India.
