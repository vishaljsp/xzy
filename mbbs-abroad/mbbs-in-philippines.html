Frequently Asked Questions for Studying MBBS in Philippines

Does NEET Require to get Admission in Philippines?

Yes. It is required to qualify for the NEET exam before getting admission to a Philippines medical college. Some universities offer admission to MBBS for students who have scored just qualification marks in the Exam. Most of the other universities ask for minimum qualifying marks in NEET exam.

What are the minimum marks required in12th PCB to get admission in Philippines Medical College?

Candidates must score at least 50% marks in PCB ( Physics, Chemistry, Biology) and English in order to get admission in any Philippines medical college.

Do I Need to qualify IELTS and TOEFL to get MBBS admission in Philippines ?

Numerous colleges in Philippines don’t expect you to qualify either TOEFL or IELTS. Notwithstanding, a few foundations do expect you to qualify one of those tests.

Is MBBS from Philippines valid for practice in India ?

MCI perceives most clinical establishments in Philippines. Degrees from those establishments are legitimate for training in India. The main necessity for them is that they should qualify the MCI Screening Test and Foreign Medical Graduates Examination (FMGE) is a licensure examination conducted by the National Board of Examination (NBE) in India.
