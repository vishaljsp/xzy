MBBS IN Kazakhstan: Fee Structure, Eligibility and Admission Process 2020

by Future Education | Sep 11, 2020

Kazakhstan is becoming a boom for international students who want to pursue their MBBS abroad. Kazakhstan has several world-class Government as well as private Medical Universities that offer MBBS, MD, and other medical degree courses.

Presently there are more than 5000 Indian students pursuing MBBS in Kazakhstan from MCI approved medical universities in Kazakhstan. The students who complete their MBBS or any other medical degree from Kazakhstan can apply for employment across the globe.

The course duration for MBBS in Kazakhstan is 5.8 years. The students are not required to pass any entrance examination to get admission to MBBS Universities in Kazakhstan. Thus MBBS in Kazakhstan has a very easy process for students dreaming to become a doctor.

Frequently Asked Questions for Studying MBBS in KAZAKHSTAN

Does NEET Require to get Admission in KAZAKHSTAN?

Yes. It is required to qualify for the NEET exam before getting admission to KAZAKHSTAN medical college. Some universities offer admission to MBBS for students who have scored just qualification marks in the Exam. Most of the other universities ask for minimum qualifying marks in NEET exam.

What are the minimum marks required in12th PCB to get admission in KAZAKHSTAN Medical College?

Candidates must score at least 50% marks in PCB ( Physics, Chemistry, Biology) and English in order to get admission in any KAZAKHSTAN medical college.

Do I Need to qualify IELTS and TOEFL to get MBBS admission in KAZAKHSTAN ?

Numerous colleges in KAZAKHSTAN don’t expect you to qualify either TOEFL or IELTS. Notwithstanding, a few foundations do expect you to qualify one of those tests.

What is the Fee Structure of Medical Colleges in KAZAKHSTAN ?

The education costs for most medical universities in KAZAKHSTAN is somewhere close to $6000 to $8000. The inn expense is about $160 to $260. The inn expense is moderately higher contrasted with the rents charged by spaces outside the colleges.

Which are Top 5 Medical Universities in KAZAKHSTAN?

Based on the world universities’ Ranking database. The following universities are the top 5 in KAZAKHSTAN for Medical Courses.

  1. KAZAKH NATIONAL MEDICAL UNIVERSITY(ALMATY)
  2. ASTANA MEDICAL UNIVERSITY
  3. SEMEY STATE MEDICAL UNIVERSITY
  4. SOUTH KAZAKHSTAN MEDICAL ACADEMY SHYMKENT
  5. West Kazakhstan State Medical Academy “Marat Ospanov”
Is MBBS from KAZAKHSTAN valid for practice in India ?

MCI perceives most clinical establishments in KAZAKHSTAN. Degrees from those establishments are legitimate for training in India. The main necessity for them is that they should qualify the MCI Screening Test and Foreign Medical Graduates Examination (FMGE) is a licensure examination conducted by the National Board of Examination (NBE) in India.
