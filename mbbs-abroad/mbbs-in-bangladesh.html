by Future Education | Sep 11, 2020

Bangladesh is becoming a boom for international students who want to pursue their MBBS abroad. Bangladesh has several world-class Government as well as private Medical Universities that offer MBBS, MD, and other medical degree courses.

Bangladesh is a good choice for Indian students. Currently, Bangladesh has 50 private and 25 public universities for medical education. It is one of the low-cost MBBS programs among foreign countries for Indian citizens. There are certain eligibility criteria that students have to clear for MBBS admission in Bangladesh.

Frequently Asked Questions for Studying MBBS in Bangladesh

Does NEET Require to get Admission in Bangladesh?

Yes. It is required to qualify for the NEET exam before getting admission to a Bangladesh medical college. Some universities offer admission to MBBS for students who have scored just qualification marks in the Exam. Most of the other universities ask for minimum qualifying marks in NEET exam.

What are the minimum marks required in12th PCB to get admission in Bangladesh Medical College?

Candidates must score at least 50% marks in PCB ( Physics, Chemistry, Biology) and English in order to get admission in any Bangladesh medical college.

Do I Need to qualify IELTS and TOEFL to get MBBS admission in Bangladesh ?

Numerous colleges in Bangladesh don’t expect you to qualify either TOEFL or IELTS. Notwithstanding, a few foundations do expect you to qualify one of those tests.

Is MBBS from Bangladesh valid for practice in India ?

MCI perceives most clinical establishments in Bangladesh. Degrees from those establishments are legitimate for training in India. The main necessity for them is that they should qualify the MCI Screening Test and Foreign Medical Graduates Examination (FMGE) is a licensure examination conducted by the National Board of Examination (NBE) in India.

Top Medical Universities in Bangladesh for MBBS

University of Dhaka

The University of Dhaka is the oldest university in Bangladesh. Nawab Sir Khwaja Salimullah played a pioneering role in establishing the university in Dhaka donated 600 acres of land from his estate for this purpose

Eligibility: 50% marks in PCB followed by an Online test.
Entrance Qualification

Rajshahi University

The University of Rajshahi, also known as Rajshahi University or RU is a public university which is the second largest university in Bangladesh, in Rajshahi, a city in northwestern Bangladesh. It was established in 1953,

Eligibility: 50% marks in PCB
Entrance: NEET Qualification

 

The University of Chittagong

The University of Chittagong is a public research university with multidisciplinary faculties situated across a 2110-acre hilly landmass in Fatehpur Union of Hathazari Upazila, 22 kilometers north of Chittagong city of Bangladesh. This university has the largest campus among Bangladeshi universities.

Eligibility: 60% marks in PCB with NEET Score
Entrance: NEET Qualification

 

Gono University

Gono University is a private university in Savar, Bangladesh which was established on 14 July 1998. It is now operating its academic and administrative activity on its permanent campus at Nalam, Savar, Dhaka. It was approved by the University Grants Commission in 1998.

Eligibility: 60% marks in PCB followed by an Online test.
Entrance: NEET Qualification

 

University of Science & Technology Chittagong

University of Science & Technology Chittagong is a private university in Bangladesh. The university was established with the sponsorship of a private charity on 13 May 1989.

Eligibility: 65% marks in PCB
Entrance: NEET Qualification
